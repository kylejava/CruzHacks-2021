What is Noteify?

Noteify is a website aimed to improve a person's mental health. Noteify allows people to write themselves notes and place them in various "jars". People can write a note talking about an accomplishment they are proud of, their goals in the future, or a funny story, anything that will help them stay positive or even just brighten up their day.

How Do I Use Noteify?

Get started with Noteify Here! Create an account with your email and you will be able to create your own notes! Clicking on a jar will show you a random note you have entered in that jar. Click on "Create A New Note" in order to start customizing your own notes and put them in any of the 5 jars.

Why Should I Use Noteify?

With the increase of depression and other various mental illnesses increasing, it is important to take this matter seriously. There is so much research discussing the link between positive thinking and a better, healthier lifestyle. Noteify helps people's mental health by giving user's a more positive way of thinking.
